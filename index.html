Apply here

Choose Beijing or Shanghai, you can apply for the 3-day program. You can attend a Welcome to Pitt workshop, which includes a session for your parents, reception with Pitt alumni, and a cultural exploration trip with current American Pitt students. During the fall semester 2018 at Pitt, you then will participate in the Pitt-to-You mentorship program on campus, which will provide you with the tools to grow and succeed during your course of study at Pitt.

Costs for tours, visits, and group meals will be covered by the University of Pittsburgh. You will be expected to pay for the cost of your transportation to Beijing or Shanghai and any required hotel accommodations.
